SafeStart Chicago

Creating Opportunities. Building Safer Communities.

Our Mission

SafeStart Chicago is a youth-led initiative dedicated to preventing gun violence through education, mentorship, and community-building. We aim to give young people in Black communities a safer, brighter future by addressing the root causes of violence and creating real opportunities.

What We Do

  • Provide after-school programs focused on personal development and safety
  • Offer mentorship opportunities with local leaders and professionals
  • Partner with organizations like UCAN and the Institute for Nonviolence
  • Host community healing events and mental health workshops

Together, we can make a difference. Become a mentor, volunteer at our events, or support our programs. Every action counts.
